Lion Air

ICAO : LNI

IATA : JT

CALLSIGN : LION INTER

Lion Air is an Indonesian low-cost airline headquartered in Jakarta. Founded in 1999, it has grown to become one of the largest budget carriers in Southeast Asia, operating both domestic and international flights. Lion Air is known for its extensive network, affordable fares, and a large fleet of modern aircraft.

Wings Air

ICAO : WON

IATA : IW

CALLSIGN : WINGS ABADI

Wings Air is a regional subsidiary of Lion Air, based in Indonesia. Established in 2003, Wings Air primarily operates short-haul domestic flights, connecting smaller cities and towns within the archipelago.

Batik Air

ICAO : BTK

IATA : ID

CALLSIGN : BATIK

Batik Air is a full-service airline based in Indonesia and is the full-service arm of the Lion Air Group. Founded in 2013, Batik Air distinguishes itself from its parent company, Lion Air, by offering a more premium and comfortable flying experience.

Thai Lion Air
